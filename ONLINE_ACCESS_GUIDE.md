## 🚀 Quick Start for Online Users

### Step 1: Open Your Browser
Use any modern browser:
- Google Chrome (Recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari

### Step 2: Go to the Website
Type the website URL in your browser's address bar.

### Step 3: Create an Account
1. Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**
2. Fill in your details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
3. Submit and wait for admin approval

### Step 4: Log In
Once approved (you'll receive an email):
1. Click **"Login"**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Start booking!

## 📱 Mobile Access

The website is mobile-responsive! Access it on your smartphone:

1. Open your mobile browser
2. Go to the website URL
3. Bookmark for easy access:
   - **iPhone:** Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
   - **Android:** Tap Menu → Add to Home Screen

## 🔒 Account Security Tips

1. **Use a strong password** (8+ characters, mix of letters & numbers)
2. **Don't share your login** credentials
3. **Log out** when using shared computers
4. **Keep your email** updated for notifications

---

## ❓ Website Down?

If you can't access the website:

1. **Check your internet connection**
2. **Try a different browser**
3. **Clear browser cache**
4. **Wait 30 minutes** and try again
5. **Use the Google Docs fallback** (link above)
6. **Contact us** via email or phone
